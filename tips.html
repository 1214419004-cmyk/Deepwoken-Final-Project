How To Progress

  1. Leveling Up

    You do this by beating monsters and using the training tools to alocate your stats.

  2. Comiting to an Oath

    An oath is a special class you can obtain by meeting its unique requirements and doing whait it asks to upgrade it allowing you to make use of its special abilities.

  3. Obtaining Resonance

    Resonance aka bell is a small bell you can unlock on your waist that activates a random useful ability when you ring it. You obtain it by beating enough bosses to fill your "bell xp bar" but that bar is invisible to the player and the only way to check if you have enough for bell is getting knocked down in a dangerous place called The Depths.
